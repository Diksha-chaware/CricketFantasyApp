Batting Points

 This is a summary of the content

This is the primary content of the panel.

 Currently I am {{panelOpenState ? 'open' : 'closed'}}

  1. Any player scoring a century will only get points for the century. No points will be awarded as their 30 run Bonus or Half-century Bonus. Additionally, no points are awarded for centuries in T10 matches.
  2. If any runs are scored on an overthrow, points for those runs will be credited to the batter on strike for that ball. However, if the overthrow goes for a boundary, the batter will not receive extra Boundary Bonus points.
